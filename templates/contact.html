Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our services

Do you provide a warranty on your repairs?

Yes, we offer a 24-month/24,000-mile warranty on all parts and labor. This warranty covers any defects in workmanship or materials for the specified period.

How long does a typical service take?

Service times vary depending on the type of maintenance or repair needed. Routine maintenance can typically be completed in 1-2 hours, while more complex repairs may take a full day or longer. We always provide an estimated completion time when you bring your vehicle in.

Do you offer loaner vehicles or shuttle service?

We offer a complimentary local shuttle service for customers within a 5-mile radius of our facility. For repairs requiring more than one day, we have a limited number of loaner vehicles available for our regular customers (subject to availability).

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, cash, and checks. We also offer financing options for larger repairs through our partnership with Synchrony Financial.

Do I need an appointment or can I just drop in?

While we do accept walk-ins for minor services, we strongly recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure prompt service. You can book an appointment online through our website, by phone, or in person.
